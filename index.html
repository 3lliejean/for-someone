Dear Muazzin,

Thank you! Yes, I'd say that again. Twice. Thrice. A Million times over. But have I ever told you the reason behind it? I may have, but heck, there are a lot of reasons. Every day, every night, every passing moment, I was and will be grateful to you. Yes, I too have this question in my mind. "What did I do to deserve this?" A human being often disgraces himself. Why? That is a question for myself. Back then, I really humbled and humiliated myself in front of myself. At one moment and another, I really thought I was a shame. A curse. I remember how I used to humiliate myself, hurt myself. Cried myself to sleep only to wake up and suffer with it all over again. I was at a point where either I had to solve this or end this misery with myself. But what could I do? Was I capable of anything?
"My Creator. My Lord. The Ever-Hearing. What did I do to deserve this? Why are you letting this all happen?"...
"I challenge you. If you're there, actually there and listening, if you really want me on your path, if it's all real, then bring me out of this misery, with any of your miracles. I don't care how and when, or I am never sitting here again, in front of you, ever again!"
And suddenly, I was free. I was over the moon, for some reason. He wasn't real. Nothing was. No hope anymore. No humiliation. I don't have to show any courage or patience to get over it. I guess I lived exactly how I wanted.
Until one midnight, I was sitting in front of someone I had known long before, for someone I had never known. I cried, but not with tears. I was sitting with a continuous pain in my heart. After a long time, I felt an emotion. Little did I know, this was that miracle. Little did I know that the Ever-Knowing, the Ever-Hearing, heard me. I was back. You gave me myself back. I know I've told you this many times before, too, but no words can ever describe the emotions and events I went through before you, and during you. And, this is just one of the many, many reasons why I am so grateful to you, for you. Tell me, if it were you instead of me, wouldn't you wish to sacrifice your everything to payback, knowing that it will still not be enough?
Then, another night, I sat in front of Him. I was pleading for forgiveness, and in the other moment, I was thanking Him for everything. I felt guilty, and in the other moment, I felt as if I were the luckiest person right now. I was calling Him again and again. I was calling you. That was when everything made sense to me, and everything that I went through felt worth it. Back then, I knew I was a great sinner. Yet, I resisted everything. I was trapped, everything was suffocating me. I knew that He didn't love me anymore. I tried never to care about it. But I did care about it. Then? "And trust me, you're the kind of person beloved to Allah, He would never leave you alone, no matter what. We don't know each other that much for now, but the more I get to know you, you're from people of Jannah with hearts of birds." Nothing is by chance, and nothing goes in vain. "Oh Muazzin! You're so lucky that He chose you" I cried. What a perfect name you have, Muazzin. The more I got close to you, the more I trusted and believed in Him, and the closer I got to Him.
To be very honest, I don't know what love is and what it's like. And to be fair, I am not in a hurry to know that. All I know is that you know it's right when you don't lose yourself, you find more of yourself.
You're ideal. You've seen the deepest, most bruised parts of me and still wished to stay. How long have we been together? Hardly a year. Yet you exposed me to myself.
I don't want to end this. I want to keep writing because it feels like I am talking to you, and one only loves to talk with someone who is listening. You gave me confidence. The confidence I never had. Now, I feel like I am living.
Don't get bored with my 'Thank You' because I will say that often from now on. And do not always seek a reason behind it because, most of the time, it's the realization that I've come so far, so far away from my problems, only because I knew you were with me no matter what. Is this love? I don't know. All I know is that no prayer, no pain, no suffering, nothing goes in vain. You made me believe this. You made me believe in love.
Oh, and the main reason behind writing all of this before stepping into the next year with you is that you made me feel seen, heard, and blessed. 2023 and 2024 were the worst years of my life. 2025? The year I will remember forever. Who knows what's coming next? All I know is that you're coming with me. I am also aware of the upcoming storms, which you and I both have to deal with. New year, new problems I guess, but it will be different this time. We have each other now. We both have someone to share our thoughts and our feelings with. To talk about our failures and celebrate our successes.
...Every precious and beautiful thing reminds me of you now. Flowers and books remind me of you a lot. That pretty sunset reminds me of you. The golden hour reminds me of you. Almost everything reminds me of you.
I expressed too much, didn't I? However, I do want you to know about my thoughts and my feelings. I just hope they don't become another burden on your shoulders.

With Love,
Abeeha
